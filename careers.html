WE ARE HIRING

Cake Decorator - Full time

We are looking for a talented decorator who has experience working with whip cream, buttercream and fondant. You will also be required to know how to follow recipes as you will be required to bake cakes and prepare fillings. Experience in customer service is required as you will be regularly communicating with customers in person, over the phone or by email to take their orders or go over any other questions that they may have. It is a fast paced environment that requires a lot of attention to detail and work well under pressure.

  • - Fondant experience
  • - Icing and covering cakes
  • - Writing and drawing on cakes
  • - Customer service
  • - Attention to detail
  • - Carry 50lbs or more
  • - Be able to follow recipes
  • - Be a team player
  • - Work well under pressure
  • - A valid California drivers license for cake deliveries

This job requires to be available to work weekends and some occasional over time, if that may be an inconvenience then this position will not be a fit for you. There will be training to get the candidate up and going but previous work experience is required. Please do not contact us by phone or in person in regards to the position, provide your resume in Word or PDF format and we will follow up with you, thank you.
