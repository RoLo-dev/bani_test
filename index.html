About

My name is Bani Dao, I enjoy doing several things such as crafts, cooking and gardening. Above all, I mostly enjoy cake decorating, it is my passion. I started cake decorating in 2009, searching on YouTube. After 2 years being a home baker making cakes for family and friends, I decided to take it further by turning it to a profession. In 2011, I graduated from Le Cordon Bleu in Baking and Patisserie. Since then, Iâ€™ve been working in several places as a pastry cook and cake decorator to gain real-life experience and knowledge about my field. From hobby to business, I continue baking cakes, pastries and now catering to the Orange County residents. Cake design has become my passion in life, so I always keep up to date with new and upcoming trends and try out new techniques to continue to improve and refine my skills.

Contact

Have any questions? Please feel free to leave any questions or comments. Place your cake orders 7-5 days prior from due date, also please provide detailed information about your cake (cake flavor, cake theme, and servings). For Sweet table events, place your orders 1 week prior from due date including events with floral arrangements. Pastry and cookie orders should be placed at least 4 days prior from due date. We do require a 50% deposit for any order over $75.
