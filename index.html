GETTING STARTED

Placing your Order

We ask our customers to please place their orders ahead of time, we try our best to fulfill as many orders as possible each week. Orders must be placed at least 8-10 days in advance. We also ask to have pictures ready of the cake design they're interested in for reference as well as the amount of servings to determine the size of the cake


Pricing

We don't have any standard pricing for our custom cakes since all custom cakes vary depending on the design and size of the cake. As for basic cakes or basic desserts, we do have standard pricing


Deposit

Yes, we do require a deposit for any order over $75, minimum deposit is 50% of the total amount of the order. Any order that does not receive a deposit will be put on hold, no deposit no cake. Deposits can be made through Venmo or Zelle


Last Minute Orders

We occasionally take last minute orders only if we are capable to squeeze it in our schedule and if the cake design is not complex. But, there will be a rush fee charge for placing the order since everything is made to order
